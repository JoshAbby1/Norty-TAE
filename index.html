How to use LM Arena (Beginner)

Important: If you want images, press Image first before anything else.

Battle

  1. Open LM Arena and press Image.
  2. Click Battle.
  3. Paste your prompt.
  4. Generate and pick the winner.
  5. Change ONE thing and try again.

Side by Side

  1. Open LM Arena and press Image.
  2. Click Side by Side.
  3. Pick a model on the left and right.
  4. Paste your prompt and generate.
  5. Compare results and keep the best.

Direct Chat

  1. Open LM Arena and press Image (for images).
  2. Click Direct Chat.
  3. Paste your prompt.
  4. Then say: “change only the outfit / scene / camera / props”.

Best way to learn: change one thing at a time so you can see what actually affects the result.

Prompt Maker

Type it simply — it turns it into a layered prompt (outfit / scene / camera / props).

Go to LM Arena

Copy prompt → open LM Arena → press Image → choose Battle / Side by Side / Direct Chat → paste.
